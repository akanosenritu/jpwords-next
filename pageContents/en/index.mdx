#### Welcome!
##### What is this site?
This site's purpose is to provide useful resources to students who aim to take JLPT (stands for Japanese Language Proficiency Test), or more generally to study Japanese.

For the time being the main resource of this site will be the vocabulary trainer.

##### About your data
If you take any exercises at this site, your practice history will be stored in the browser (more specifically a storage called LocalStorage). By creating and logging into an account, the data will also be saved in the server.
